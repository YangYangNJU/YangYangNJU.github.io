人在不同的位置会有不同的想法，比如，学生听到导师说那谁假期也来办公室做科研，你怎么不来，心里会想真是黑心资本家呀。工作后没有人要求你假期做科研，但你观察到老板早八晚十的科研作息，听说院士每天8点就已经在食堂吃完了早饭，并且晚上很晚离开办公室。亦或者听说资深前辈把看文献当休息。你会不会想起这句：比你聪明的人还比你努力。
聪明和努力之间需要一些连接，比如自我驱动力和目标。你身边一定有一些刚开始很努力但怎么也不见有成果，后来碌碌无为的人，说明只有努力不够。努力就像燃料，积累越来越多的燃料，朝着对的方向，可以达到目标，不然也可能爆炸，受到抑制甚至会熄灭。因此，需要自驱力，努力朝着自己目标的方向。自驱力和目标就需要聪明的脑阔做决策。
如何获得目标和自驱力呢？设置具体的目标，长远目标和具体的小目标，小目标更重要，收获小目标会给你正反馈，增加自驱力，总之，一口吃不成胖子，大部分现代人都有经验。但有时候你会感觉很疲惫，不要埋怨自己，或许身体在给你信号，你是不是很久没有训练了？你是不是有社交困惑了？不要用简单的懒给自己定义。耐心和练习，你会发现自己的变化。
本文中的成功请自己定义。

Does someone tell you “the one, who is smarter than you, is more struggling than you.”

Code 1:


People in different positions have different perspectives. For example, 
if mentors ask their students what they do on weekends while their peers are working, 
the students may feel suppressed, thinking they have a demanding boss. 
Working in an academic institution, no one explicitly asks you to work on weekends. 
However, you may notice your boss working from 8 am to 10 pm. Even the director 
starts work at 8 am, although the official work time begins at 9 am. Additionally, 
some senior scientists consider reading papers as relaxation. 
This brings to mind the saying, "the one who is smarter than you is more struggling than you."


There is a connection between being smart and struggling, as it involves self-motivation 
and goals. You may know someone who is incredibly hardworking but does not achieve success. 
This implies that being driven alone is not enough. Struggling is like fuel; 
you need to accumulate enough fuel to propel yourself in the direction of your goals. 
Otherwise, burnout or failure may occur. Hence, you need self-motivation to navigate 
yourself in the right direction, utilizing your intelligence to make informed choices.


How can you achieve goals and cultivate self-motivation? It is essential to plan both 
big and small goals, with particular emphasis on the small or detailed ones. 
By achieving small goals, you gain self-confidence and receive positive feedback 
that fuels your motivation. This process is like ascending a step ladder. 
At times, you may feel tired. Instead of viewing yourself as lazy, consider whether 
your body is signaling a need for exercise or if there are social troubles that 
require attention. Be patient and practice self-reflection; 
you will witness your own transformation.


Define success for yourself.
