Research Summary

My main research interests are time domain astronomy and observational high energy astrophysics. I am part of the Zwicky Transient Facility (ZTF) and NuSTAR teams. In the past, I have worked on supernovae, X-ray binaries, and stars. For my PhD thesis, I would like to explore the dynamic sky using ZTF & the Spectrum Röntgen Gamma (SRG) X-ray mission, with a particular focus on tidal disruption events and stellar explosions with intensive end-of-life mass-loss.
